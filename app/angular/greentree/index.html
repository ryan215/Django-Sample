GreenTree Foods by EverFit is a Health Conscious Brand designed by Nutritionists, Parents, and the amazing GreenTree Chefs to bring the world a unique palette of Easy-Made, All-Natural, Always Tasty meals and snacks. With the hustle and bustle of today’s society... balancing work, family-life, and eating healthy, can often times be quite the challenge so we thought we’d make life just a little bit simpler... and greener! The GreenTree pantry will offer a variety of well-balanced meals for the whole family, including vitamin packed lunches and snacks for the kiddos! All GreenTree foods are gluten, sugar, sodium and preservative free... if we can’t pronounce it, its’ not going in our food! Our meals are prepared fresh with never frozen, all-natural ingredients, always made when ordered, and then conveniently delivered to your doorstep!

Our mission is to simply provide the World a convenient source of quality nutrition, while giving back through participation in Community Outreach Programs, Charity Events, and a lifetime of support for the Arbor Day Foundation and AmericanForests.Org to help the conservation and preservation of the American Forests. A healthy Earth produces healthy foods. Healthy foods produce healthy lives, and healthy lives complete the circle. Thank you so much for your interest in GreenTree and we’re truly excited about serving you!
