what do i owe my clients?

As a fitness professional, your friends, family and clientele look up to you for guidance through the vast amount of fitness information. The more active you are on your blog, and client interactions, ultimately the more successful you will be.

Potential clients will have the ability to reach out to you via messaging and the “Train with Me” button located on your profile page. This button will become visible when you have selected “Accepting Clients?” in your profile settings.

As a promoter, your audience will all interact with you via messaging and blog comments. Share and grow your following and income by sharing the link via the “AFFILIATE” button on your profile page.

You can turn ON or OFF your online Training ability at any time inside of your Settings.
